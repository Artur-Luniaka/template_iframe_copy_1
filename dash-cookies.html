How we use cookies in Idle Bank Game

Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our financial simulation. They help remember your progress and preferences, making your banking experience smoother when you return.

How We Use Cookies

This banking simulation uses cookies to track your financial progress and optimize gameplay. As a portfolio project, cookies demonstrate how real banking apps might store session data.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for core banking functions like saving your portfolio balance and transaction history between sessions.
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous data about how players interact with investment interfaces to improve gameplay flow.
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferred trading strategies and UI customizations for a personalized experience.

Managing Cookies

You can delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that clearing them will reset your in-game financial progress since this demo lacks server-side saving.

Third-Party Cookies

This financial simulation doesn't use third-party tracking. All cookie functionality is self-contained for educational demonstration purposes.

Updates to This Policy

While real banking apps frequently update policies, this demo's cookie usage remains static as part of its educational nature.

Contact Information

As a portfolio project, Idle Bank Game doesn't collect real user data. All cookie implementations are hypothetical demonstrations of financial app functionality.
